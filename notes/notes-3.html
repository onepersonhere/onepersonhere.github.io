CP2106 Independent Software Development Project (Orbital) 
Final Grade: CS (Artemis)
Link
Review

NUS Orbital 2022 was very fun and engaging.

Both my friend and I teamed up to build a game from scratch; at first we had troubles with choosing the correct game engine, however we have decided on Godot engine as we believe its the easiest engine to learn in the short span of 3 months (compared to Unity or Unreal) and we would spent more time building the game rather than learning the engine.

Thankfully, our Artemis level has been maintained throughout the 3 milestones. This was largely due to the fact that I had put in a lot of effort on the documentation of the game itself.
